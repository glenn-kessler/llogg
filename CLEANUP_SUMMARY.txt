================================================================================
                                 DEPLOYMENT
================================================================================

FOR ROOT DEPLOYMENT (https://your-domain.com/):
  Upload contents of public/ folder to server root

FOR SUBFOLDER DEPLOYMENT (https://your-domain.com/llogg/):
  1. Upload contents of public/ folder to /llogg/ on server
  2. Replace /llogg/index.html with index-subfolder.html
  3. Replace /llogg/sw.js with sw-subfolder.js
  4. Replace /llogg/manifest.json with manifest-subfolder.json
  5. On smartphone: Uninstall old app, clear cache, reinstall
